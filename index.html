Ethos

Principles that shape every feature decision.

One focus beats ten intentions

Clarity creates momentum. We prioritise one commitment you can genuinely carry into daily life.

Consistency is the strategy

We design for repeatability — especially when life gets busy, messy, or interrupted.

Calm is a feature

No guilt loops. No noisy dashboards. It should feel like a steady coach, not a judgemental scorecard.

Data should reduce confusion

We use metrics to explain patterns — not to overwhelm you with charts you’ll ignore.

Your agency comes first

Suggestions are optional. You stay in control of meaning, goals, and direction.

Privacy by default

We minimise data collection and aim to keep user data on-device / in the user’s iCloud where possible.
