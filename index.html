How we’re trying to work

Early principles that guide our decisions — and that we expect to keep refining.

Start with one clear focus

We’ve found that reducing scope often increases follow-through. OneThing starts with what matters most, not everything that could matter.

Design for consistency

Progress usually comes from repeatable actions. We aim to support habits people can return to, even when motivation dips.

Keep things calm by default

We try to avoid noise, guilt, and unnecessary prompts. If something adds pressure without clarity, it probably doesn’t belong.

Learn carefully through pilots

Both apps are being shaped through small pilots and feedback. We’d rather get the foundations right than rush features.
