Ethos

Principles that shape every feature decision.

One focus beats ten intentions

Clarity creates momentum. We prioritise a single commitment you can actually carry into daily life.

Consistency is the strategy

We design for repeatability — especially when life gets busy, messy, or interrupted.

Calm is a feature

No guilt loops. No noisy dashboards. It should feel like a steady coach, not a judgemental scorecard.

Data should reduce confusion

We use metrics to explain patterns — not to overwhelm you with charts you’ll ignore.

Your agency comes first

Suggestions are optional. You stay in control of meaning, goals, and direction.

Privacy by default

We minimise data collection and aim to keep user data on-device / in the user’s iCloud where possible.

Roadmap

Transparent, flexible, and realistic. Dates only when we’re sure.

Now

Foundations

  • Clear “home” experience: what matters this week, and how you’re doing
  • Editable OneThing statement
  • Consistency tracking + streaks (simple, honest)
  • Backup/export safety

Next

Useful insight

  • Weekly summaries: wins, misses, patterns, one recommendation
  • Contextual reflections anchored to your OneThing
  • Health integrations where they genuinely help
  • Better onboarding: inspiring-but-achievable goals

Later

Depth (only if it stays calm)

  • Smarter “coach” commentary referencing your OneThing
  • Optional programmes/challenges (time-boxed, realistic)
  • Custom dashboards that still feel simple
  • Community features only if they reduce friction

Roadmap rule: if a feature adds noise, it doesn’t ship.
