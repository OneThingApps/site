How it works

A simple loop you can repeat in real life.

Pick the OneThing

A short statement that matters — and feels achievable.

Show up

Track only what supports consistency. No clutter, no guilt loops.

Notice friction

When it slips, we’re curious — not judgemental. We remove obstacles and try again.

Review weekly

A calm summary: what worked, what didn’t, and one small adjustment.

Roadmap (Habit)

What we’re validating during the pilot, and what comes next.

Now (pilot focus)

  • Does the OneThing concept feel clear and motivating in daily life?
  • Consistency tracking that feels supportive, not nagging
  • Home screen: “what matters this week and how I’m doing”
  • Reliability: no data loss, clean sync behaviour

Next (post-pilot)

  • Refined onboarding based on pilot feedback
  • Weekly summaries and reflections
  • Polish, performance, accessibility
  • Public release plan (only when it feels solid)

Rule: if it adds noise, it doesn’t ship.
